Our Story

Miricl is more than just a drink — it's a movement towards healthier, cleaner, and more enjoyable hydration.

Founded by a team passionate about wellness and innovation, Miricl started with a bold vision: to redefine hydration using natural ingredients, clean formulations, and unforgettable taste. Each beverage we craft is free from artificial sweeteners, packed with real flavor, and designed to fuel your day without compromise.

From the vibrant tang of citrus to exotic blends and smooth classics, our drinks support your lifestyle — whether you're working out, unwinding, or powering through your passion. Join us in drinking different.
